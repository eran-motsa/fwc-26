How points are calculated

Odds multiplier — multiplies the direction odds (1/X/2). Example: away win at 5.7 with a ×2 multiplier pays 11.4.
Exact bonus — flat points added on top when your exact score is right.
points = direction_odds × multiplier ( + exact_bonus if exact )
